Welcome to the Sentence-to-Password Creator

                             by Jonathan Acevedo

   This tool takes a full sentence and produces a decryption key and password. The key produced can be later used to decrypt your password in case you forget it. The sentence could be anything from lyrics, favorite movie quotes, book quotes, any memorable quote, etc. The password complexity will depend on the amount of words in the sentence. This tool is best useful if you can remember the full sentence, but it's okay if you need to store it alongside the key.


   The purpose of this application is to better the way we create passwords, improve the security of password protected accounts worldwide and encourage the use of secure, complex & impersonal passwords. Remembering a password is often dificult for most people and we tend to store it somewhere. The problem is that when we store it, we risk it being discovered by a malicious user trying to steal your account. If we instead store information used to calculate our password, the "hacker" would have a reasonably harder time trying to figure it out.


   You might say, "Why should I try to remember a sentence when I can't even remember a password?" My answer; If you really don't have a favorite quote, song or lyric that you can remember, you can just save the sentence & key together. Saving those is safer than saving a password since the key & sentence would be useless to someone who doesn't know which method you used to create your password.


   Passwords should be smart, relatively easy to remember & hard to guess. In other words, if you have a cat, you probably shouldn't use its name in your password since someone who is actively trying to access your account would resort to many methods, including guessing your password based on your interests, public personal information(social media pictures, posts, etc) or other online activity. So, go ahead. Make a password. It's free and always will be.

To create a password:

Tip: (The more words the sentence has, the longer your password)

         1. Type a sentence into the textbox.
         2. Click the Encrypt button to create a password & key.
         3. Copy your key & sentence to a secure place.
Please enter a sentence you can remember. (Lyrics, Quotes, Long movie titles Etc.):

To decrypt your password:

         1. Type your sentence into the textbox.
         2. Type your key into the second textbox.
         3. Click the decrypt button.
         4. Copy & use your password.
